<h1><strong>Vehicle details</strong></h1>

<p>
The <strong>vehicle details</strong> window gives detailed information about a particular vehicle type.
</p>

<p>
To open: click on an individual vehicle in the <a href="vehiclelist.txt">vehicle list</a> window.
</p>

<p>
The capacity, accommodation class, <a href="comfort.txt">comfort</a>, loading time and overcrowded capacity (if applicable) are always shown.
</p>

<p>
The <strong>vehicle details</strong> window has tabs for the specifications, price and mainenance information, and a list of selectable livery schemes.
</p>
